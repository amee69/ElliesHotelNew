At Hotel Ellies, every minute is an opportunity to make priceless memories. Set out on an adventure while relaxing. Visit the neighboring turtle hatchery to experience the miracle of new life as newborn turtles hatch and start their journey. Explore the treasures both inside and outside of our walls. Take part in the time-honored activity of fishing, whether it's for the excitement of the catch or the peace of the activity. Back at the hotel, the pool is more than just a place to cool down; it's also a place to have a good time and laugh with your friends and family. A great balance of excitement and relaxation is guaranteed throughout your stay at Hotel Ellies, with a mix of thrilling adventures and relaxing downtime.

Swimming pool

Enter a world of adventure at Hotel Ellies' tranquil poolside. Our pool is more than just a body of water; it's a story waiting to happen. Swim in the cool water's shimmering depths or play a thrilling game of water volleyball with your buddies. For the brave, make a happy splash as you down our twisting water slides. After all the activity has worked up an appetite, enjoy some delectable nibbles and cool drinks from our poolside service. Our pool area is intended to be your playground for adventures, whether you're looking for heart-pounding excitement or serene relaxation.







Turtle Hatchery

Steps away from our restaurant, at Hotel Ellies, you'll find a fascinating turtle hatchery. Observe in awe as tiny turtle hatchlings set out on their life's journey,\ assisted by fervent conservation efforts. This touching encounter puts you in close proximity to nature's wonders and serves as a reminder for everyone of the value of safeguarding these magnificent animals. Your trip to the turtle hatchery is a special chance to get close to marine life and support a sustainable future.
